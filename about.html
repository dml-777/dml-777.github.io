About Me

I am a photographer and creative coder exploring atmosphere, texture, and everyday moments through film, digital photography, and interactive work. My images focus on flash, movement, and candid scenes, capturing spaces and people in a way that feels both personal and composed. Across my work, I’m interested in how visual storytelling can hold emotion, memory, and small details that might otherwise go unnoticed.

My practice moves between 35mm film and digital media, as well as p5.js sketches that experiment with motion and interaction. I approach each piece as a study in composition and mood, often focusing on the balance between spontaneity and control. Whether through photography or code, my work is driven by observation, experimentation, and a strong attention to visual detail.

My Work

This portfolio is organized into film photography, digital photography, and interactive digital art. Together, these sections reflect my interest in image-making across both traditional and computational forms.

My work often explores texture, flash, color, and the emotional tone of ordinary moments, with a visual language shaped by observation, experimentation, and atmosphere.
