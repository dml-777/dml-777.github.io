Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is a classic chocolate chip cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix ingredients together
  3. Scoop dough onto baking sheet
  4. Bake for 12 minutes
