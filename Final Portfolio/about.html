About Me

My work moves between 35mm film, digital photography, and creative coding. I’m drawn to flash, movement, reflections, color, and candid moments that feel personal but still carefully composed.

I use photography as a way to hold onto atmosphere — the feeling of a room, a night out, a passing detail, or a moment that would otherwise disappear quickly.

Creative Practice

This portfolio brings together film photography, digital photography, and experimental visual work. Across each section, I focus on mood, texture, composition, and storytelling.

Whether I’m shooting on film, using a digital camera, or building with code, I’m interested in creating images that feel atmospheric, intimate, and visually detailed.
